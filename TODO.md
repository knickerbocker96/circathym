# Circathym AI — BeaverHacks Winning Deliverables

## Goal

Make the demo prove this sentence in the first 20 seconds:

> Generic sleep apps assume everyone has 90-minute cycles. Circathym learns your rhythm from wake quality and turns it into personalized bedtime decisions.
